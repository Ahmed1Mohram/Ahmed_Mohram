Welcome Sound Instructions
===========================

Please add a welcome sound file named "welcome-sound.mp3" to this public directory.

The sound should be:
- A professional, elegant welcome chime or tone
- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Format: MP3
- Volume: Moderate (will be played at 50% volume)

Recommended sound characteristics:
- Luxurious and sophisticated
- Gold/premium feeling
- Smooth fade in/out
- Not too loud or jarring

You can find royalty-free premium sounds at:
- Freesound.org
- Zapsplat.com
- Mixkit.co
- YouTube Audio Library

Search for: "luxury chime", "golden bell", "premium notification", "elegant intro"
